Open positions: Helsinki

iOS or Android Developer (Helsinki)

You remember the feeling when you first saw a mobile app you created running on an actual device. You took the app with you and showed it around. There's something magical about it. And you still get the same kicks (even if you don't necessarily say it out loud) when your app is approved in store, or you get that five star reviews from strangers. No matter how many times you've done that, it's always a great feeling to deliver a quality app to users.

Mobile apps have changed the world, and they have definitely changed your life. While smartphones are just tools and toys for others, for you they are platforms for creating digital services that people love. You've been working with at least Android or iOS for some time, and probably other ecosystems as well.

Now you are looking for a new challenge. You want to be working with the best developers and designers out there. You want to have colleagues with whom you can have meaningful conversations about technologies and the world around them. You can definitely show them a thing or two about development, and are also eager to learn from them.

We're looking for passionate Android and iOS developers who want to have an impact on people's lives, love new challenges and enjoy life on the edge. We can offer you challenging work and skilled colleagues you can learn from.

To convince us, don't just tell where you have worked or studied and what have you done. Tell us who you are and where you are heading at. Describe what you already know, and what you will learn next. You might also take a look at how to make a great application for more inspiration.

What awaits you:

  • Concepting digital services in an interdisciplinary team, together with our clients from various industries
  • Development of top-notch native apps for Android or iOS and watchOS
  • Sharing experiences and learnings with your colleagues
  • Constant learning and self-improvement through conferences and team events
  • Flat hierarchy where your voice will be heard

What we expect:

  • You are fluent in English. It doesn't hurt if you also speak Finnish.
  • You have published several well rated apps in app stores. They can be work or hobby projects.
  • You have worked as a part of development teams using agile methodologies.
  • You have a good understanding of the similarities and differencies between other platforms, as well as future trends.
  • You have a passion and skill required to build and ship high-quality software.
  • You can describe the pros and cons of choosing between native and cross-platform apps. And you can make the decision in the beginning of the project.
  • You have a strong command of the development frameworks, toolset and popular third-party libraries.
  • It's a plus if you have experience with Swift [iOS], reactive frameworks (e.g. Rx, ReactiveCocoa), modern architectures (e.g. MVC, MVVM, VIPER) and dependency injection.
  • Familiarity with testing tools and judgment to write concise, effective unit tests.
  • You are able to discuss project work with our customers, over phone, video conference, or at our or their premises. Some travelling may be required.

What's in it for you:

Probably the best part about working at Futurice are the colleagues. You get to work with some of the most brilliant and talented professionals of the digital world. In Futurice, software engineers work closely together with our designers, analysts, and business consultants, as well as with our customers. Our aim is to grow the next generation of digital leaders, and that's why this is the place to be for learning.

We also offer a competitive compensation, and a work culture that has been awarded internationally. Our core values are care, trust, transparency, and continuous improvement that we try to embrace and share as much as possible.

If you feel like you might be a match for us, check out our five steps to a great application article, and submit yours with the form below. We hope to hear from you soon!
