Open positions: Helsinki

Senior Service Designer

Do you love creating unique, world-class user experiences? Do you want to help customers meet the age of digitalitation head-on and prosper through digital distruption? Together with our customers, we work to co-create the services of the future and we want you to strengthen our forces.

Our design team offers a wide range of services, from business and service design to UX and visual design. Your job involves defining business goals and structure digital services. You'll arrange co-creation sessions and workshops with our customers, as well as work closely with other designers and developers to create game-changing digital services. You understand how the human mind works and you use your knowledge to combine complex scenarios consisting of business needs and technical opportunities as well as restrictions to create products that are a pleasure ot interact with.

Futurice is a lean service creation company. Take a look at what we do and what we've done. We offer an award-winning work environment and international customers with game-changing projects to work on. Best of all, we offer colleagues that are as talented as they are inspiring.

If you're wondering how to make your application grab our attention, read this blog post. Polish up your digital portfolio, but don't just concentrate on where you have worked or what you have done. Tell us about your passion and where are you going in your career.

Our expectations are:

  • Years of experience in creating digital services on a variety of different platforms
  • Plenty of experience in organising succesful and productive workshops
  • Good communication skills and lots of experience with working closely together with customers
  • Fluent English skills. It doesn't hurt if you know some Finnish, too.

If you're this person, send us an application using the form below.
