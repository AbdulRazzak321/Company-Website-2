Open positions: Helsinki

Cloud Software Engineer

“Why is it running in your own datacenter?” A question you have asked many times before. You might even have convinced and helped someone to have their service relying on a cloud service instead. Getting a new environment running with one command is something that makes you tingle with excitement.

Futurice is constantly looking for more engineers to create the digital services of the future. We offer you a great workplace with awesome colleagues. We strive to create an environment where you can grow as a professional and take your talent further.

We trust our employees and care for their well-being. We have a transparent culture, where we welcome everyone to take part in discussions on organisational development.

To convince us that you are one of us, don't just tell us where you have worked or studied. Tell us who you are, and where you are heading. Describe what you already know, and what you will learn next. You might also want to take a look at how to make a great application for more inspiration.

Here's a little checklist before you apply:

  • You have graduated, or you are able to work at least four days a week.
  • You are fluent in English. It doesn't hurt if you can also speak Finnish fluently.
  • You know how to write Hello World in multiple programming languages. Actually, you have for years.
  • You can connect to a REST API. And you can create one if needed.
  • You know how to enable smooth automated deployment with the click of a button (or less), and why one should do it.
  • You have dipped your toes in more than one cloud service, and can claim to be an expert in at least one.
  • You can convincingly argue why to use a cloud service and why it is ok to use them.
  • You know that cloud services are not just for computing, and have experience of several user facing cloud-enabled apps already.
  • You follow and preferably participate in the developer community actively.

All in all, we are not just looking for a person for a specific position. We are looking for people who know their stuff, and want to learn more.
