Open positions: Tampere

UI/Visual Designer (Tampere)

Do you love creating unique world class UI visuals with great UX? For our success it's critical that the services we build have exceptionally good user experience and outstanding visual design.

As UI/Visual Designer, we expect you to be able to produce high quality UI visuals and assets as well as to work as a member of multidisciplinary design & development team to polish the final product to deliver the highest standard of UX.

All team members have a wide range of responsibilities and are involved in every part of the design and development process, therefore positivity and a ‘can do’ attitude are crucial. Individuals are given the freedom to deliver the highest-standard of work in the best way possible, producing outstanding game-changing digital products and services in collaboration with ambitious international clients.

Mutual respect is also an essential condition, as is passion, talent and energy. All team members need to be ambitious self-starters and have a desire to be part of a workplace with a lean, flat structure, built on a foundation of Nordic straightforwardness. Individuals are constantly given opportunities to learn and grow in collaboration with wonderfully nice and extremely talented people.

Above all Designers need to be excited by beautifully designed and innovative, cross media end-to-end digital experiences that have a positive impact on people’s lives and deliver real purpose for the customer.

Take a look at what we do and what we've done and get in touch. Don’t just tell us where you have worked or studied, tell us who you are and where you are heading and what you can bring to the Futurice design team. Describe what you already know and what you want to learn next and take a look at this blog post.

A digital portfolio with examples of successfully launched projects that demonstrate creative visual thinking reflecting an extensive and comprehensive understanding of the design process is a crucial accompaniment to your application.

Skills

  • A creative thinker, able to inspire team members and customers with visually compelling, well-thought out design solutions.
  • Working knowledge of the entire creative design process, including some understading of UI development and hands-on digital creative and interactive media experience.
  • A resourceful nature, working around obstacles to move thing forward.
  • Impeccable, pixel-perfect attention to detail.
  • Enthusiasm for digital design best practice, new interaction models, design standards and emerging technologies.
  • Interest and participation in professional industry groups, conferences, speaking engagements, publications, etc.
  • High-level competency in illustration, video and motion graphics design skills (editing and animation) would be a distinct advantage.
  • Fluent Finnish is a big plus for our local customers

Experience

  • Proven experience of accountability in collaboration with a cross-functional agile team.
  • Some commercial exposure of designing the highest standard of digital services for a variety of platforms, e.g., websites, software applications, mobile and tablet UI, TV set-top box, experiential installations, gesture-based interaction models, etc.
  • Practical working knowledge of agile development methodology.
  • Some understanding and exposure to the technical side of digital development and delivery.
  • A digital portfolio with several examples of successfully launched projects that demonstrate creative visual thinking reflecting an understanding of the design process.
