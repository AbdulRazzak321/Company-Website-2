Open positions: Tampere

Backend Web Technologist (Tampere)

Do you know how to create a beatiful REST API? (Or do you prefer GraphQL instead?) Excellent! We are looking for guys and gals with world-class backend development skills to create the digital services of the future.

You are fluent with either NodeJS or the JVM ecosystem (think Java, Scala, Clojure), but not afraid of getting your hands dirty on the other side of the fence, either. At the end of the day, what matters to you is solving problems elegantly and having fun doing it!

You know how to integrate services with each other, have them tested automatically in a CI environment, and deploy them into the cloud: AWS, Heroku, or maybe Azure. You know why it's good to have a proper staging environment set up, and know the difference a good developer documentation makes.

You like to surround yourself and collaborate with motivated people of various skills and backgrounds, learn more and get better at what you do every day. You're aware of the recent discoveries in web and server technologies and know what's happening in the developer community.

We offer you a great workplace with awesome colleagues, interesting projects with the possibility of working at Futurice offices abroad for shorter or longer stints, excellent tools and benefits, and great possibilities for continuous learning and professional growth. We are proud of our lean, flat organization and distinctive organizational culture – one that has been selected as the best place to work in Europe, twice.

Fluent Finnish is a big plus due to our local customers.

If you feel like you've just been described above, are a nice guy or gal who is absolutely fun to work with, and would like to work with some of the nicest & most talented people in the industry, we would love to get your application! To apply, just fill out the form below.

For some great tips on what to put in your application, Pette has got you covered.
