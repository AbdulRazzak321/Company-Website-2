Slush x Jobspotting TOA Thursday Afterparty

Slush x Jobspotting TOA Thursday Afterparty

When
16 Jul 22:00 — 17 Jul 06:00
Where
Neue Heimat, Revaler Straße 57
Berlin
Germany
Website
http://bit.ly/1JYB9CU

Slush and Jobspotting (sponsored bu Futurice and V14) are coming together to host the party of the decade at Tech Open Air Berlin, combining the awesome powers of the biggest startup event in the Nordics, with the local flavour of the most exciting new startup in Berlin.

Berlin and the Nordics are best known for their startups, tech talent and party skills and we’ll be fusing the best of both worlds on July 16th. Please join us at Neue Heimat to experience how to party Northern Europe style, meet recruiting companies and hang out with great people. The venue doors will open at at10PM and the party will continue throughout the night.

Slush X Jobspotting will make you dance ’til dawn. Here’s a preview of the DJs that will keep you on the floor all night long.

Here’s a preview of the artists that will keep you on the floor all night long.

Stage 1:

10pm until 1am: Wolf Kolster

Veteran DJ Wolf Kolster will get the party started. His blend of electro and house beats have been making Germany's bright and beautiful move to the music for years. We defy you to resist.

1am until 3am: Hessam

Jobspotting founder Hessam will hit the decks at 1am to break it down with some Hip Hop and R’n’B tunes. His beats will bring through to the ‘witching hour’.

Stage 2:

12.15am: Katie Armstrong

Get a little artistic after midnight with performance, video and collage artist Katie Armstrong. This resident of Berlin’s famous Eigen+Art Lab creates a blend of high art and high tech that will let you taste of the complete Berlin experience.

1am until 3am: Christopher Breuer & James Martin Lust Garten

These guys worked with the legendary Oliver Koletzki and regularly spin at Kantine am Berghain / Panorama Bar (OFFICIAL), Berlin’s most famous club. Make sure to catch SvT for the real Hauptstadt experience.

3am until dawn: J.Lindroos (Dept.)

J.Lindroos is the main man behind Helsinki based record label Dept. He’s here to show you how the Nordics do it. We may not havemidnight sun in Berlin, but his house and techno will help you greet the morning in style.

You're welcome to attend the FB event and invite all your friends!
