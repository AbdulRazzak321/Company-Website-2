About the IxDA:

The Interaction Design Association (IxDA) is the largest professional association of interaction designers in the world. With over 65,000 members and more than 152 local groups in 43 countries, IxDA´s focus is to work with interaction design issues for professionals, regardless of their level of experience. IxDA was founded in 2003 and registered as a not for profit organization in late 2005. We depend on the passion of the members as volunteers to help serve the international Interaction Design community needs.

About IxDA Helsinki chapter:

IxDA Helsinki is the local Finnish chapter for Interaction Design Association IxDA. We're a non-profit, loosely organized professional community. Our aim is to connect the local design community and promote modern interaction design practices in Finland through monthly meetups and other activities. Currently, we have approximately 1660 members in our Facebook community, there we share our events and discuss design related content. We are also hosting and organizing the global Interaction 16 conference on March next year.

To know more about the conference and association go to http://www.ixda.org or more about our local chapter on http://interaction16.ixda.org/ixda-helsinki/
