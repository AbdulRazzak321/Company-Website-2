:clojureD

:clojureD

When
24 Jan 09:00 — 24 Jan 20:00
Where
Beuth Hochschule für Technik
Berlin
Germany
Website
http://www.clojured.de/

 

After a successful Euroclojure Berlin 2013 with the help of the German team, we do need more new independent conferences in Germany. The :clojureD conference is held Saturday the 24th of January in Berlin in cooperation with the BOB Conference one day earlier.

The focus is pure Clojure, new technologies and interesting developments in the entire Clojure ecosystem. We want to cover the entire spectrum from beginner to advanced developer while focussing on pure Clojure: from the beginner, to advanced developers, who are also e.g. interested in related topics as ClojureScript.
