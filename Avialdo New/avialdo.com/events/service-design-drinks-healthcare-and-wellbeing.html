Service Design Meet Up Tampere: Digital Future of Healthcare

Service Design Meet Up: Digital Future of Healthcare

When
19 Jan 17:00 — 19 Jan 20:00
Where
Ravintola Laterna, Puutarhakatu 11
Tampere
Finland

 

Futurice will be hosting Tampere’s first service design meetup on January 19th at Ravintola Laterna.


The topic of the night will be healthcare digital trends. As an introduction for the topic Futurice will present the Health Care Trend Deck. Keynote speaker Marika Järvinen from FinnMedi Oy will give insights on the topic. During the night we will also hear a talk by Tuomas Otala, CIO of Pihlajalinna. At the end of the event we have a panel discussion about Digital trends of Healthcare and Service Design with four interesting and diverse participants.


NOTE! All presentations and the panel will be in Finnish.

About the Service Design Network SDN:

Service Design Network is the leading institution for expertise in service design, a partnership of professionals and an open-minded, knowledge-sharing network. We connect people with multiple roles within agencies, business, and government.

More than 30,000 people are now following the activities of the Service Design Network. Become part of a strong network and actively strengthen service design in both the private and the public sector.

To know more about the SDN go to http://www.service-design-network.org or more about our Finnish chapter on http://finland.service-design-network.org
