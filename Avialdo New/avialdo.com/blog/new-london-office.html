Silicon Roundabout, here we come! Futurice London is moving.

Tom McQueen Managing Director, UK

Today we move to our new space in Old Street, where we’ll be part of cluster in the very heart of tech innovation in Europe. Tech City in East London is as close as any area outside of Silicon Valley achieving this density of tech companies. Our office is located at Turnpoint London, 26 Underwood Street N1, right in this rapidly developing neighbourhood and a short walk from Silicon Roundabout.

When we moved to Sun Street in Shoreditch in March 2014, there were four of us. Now, less than two years later, we are 20 and we’re in full swing, helping companies create new services, startups create new business and traditional industries handle digital disruption every day.

Our new space will be located on the two top floors of the building, which has been completely refurbished with exposed ceilings, bike storage, showers and a private roof terrace. It will give our growing team of technologists, designers and consultants room to do what they do best – get together in small teams, collaborate with customers, code, design end-to-end services and share their insights and knowledge with each other and the world.

Having twice won Europe’s Best Place to work, over the years we’ve come to appreciate the impact the working environment has on people’s lives. So we designed the layout and space ourselves and worked with Square One Design & Build to create an inspiring place for our people, customers and collaborators. We designed our own space in Helsinki in March, so we know designing a space to fit and enhance the way we work really pays off.

We will be in our new fourth floor on Monday. With the fifth floor being completed in Spring next year, we look forward to opening the doors to our roof terrace and letting the sun, and the world, in.
