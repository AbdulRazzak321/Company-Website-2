Pekka Tarjanne - 1937-2010

Tuomas Syrjänen CEO

With deep sorrow we announce the loss of our former long time Chairman of the Board, Pekka Tarjanne. Pekka courageously joined Futurice's Board of Directors year 2001 when few believed in the young company's future. Pekka brought his extraordinary character, credibility, and leadership to help guide Futurice between years 2001-2008. We remember Pekka with great gratitude, warmth, and respect.
