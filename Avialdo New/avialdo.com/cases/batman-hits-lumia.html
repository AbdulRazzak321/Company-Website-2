Batman hits Lumia

Of all the gadgets we use every day, the mobile phone is the closest to us - both physically and psychologically. It travels with us almost everywhere we go and in many cases we rely heavily on the phone to perform cognitive functions we used to handle ourselves - like memory. 

So more than any other gadget we use, the mobile phone can reflect our personality. The #TileArt app can help.

The original concept for the #TileArt app was created by Ricc Webb aka Tile Art Guy. Futurice took the concept and developed it further into an actual application. It takes the already highly customizable look of the Windows Phone and its dynamic Live Tiles to a whole new level, allowing users to use their own images and exploit the parallax effect to give screen a layered feel. The Joker Lumia 930 Tile Art Theme is an excellent exampe.

For more information on Tile Art Guy, check out this interview and his blog.
