I am Sage Ross. I'm an admin here on Wikimedia Commons. I also edit as Sage (Wiki Ed), in my staff role with Wiki Education Foundation.

In 2010 and 2011, I worked for the Wikimedia Foundation as the Online Faciliator for the Public Policy Initiative. My main volunteer account is Ragesoss on English Wikipedia; my WMF staff accounts were Sage Ross (WMF) and Sross (Public Policy). You can reach me by email at sross (at) wikimedia.org.
